Who Are We?

We are Arjun and Yutthana from AY and Co.


AY and Co was founded on February 1st 2022, with the growth of young investor taking interest the stock market. We are a Web Development student building working together to build a functional stock market tracker using API, local storage, Tailwind CSS and JavaScript. AY and Co believe building this current market tracker can be beneficial to beginner investor who are looking to dip their toe into this sector. Our main objective is to create an easy-to-use stock market price tracker with current breaking news to enable beginner trader to track of the current news and stock market price. Our market Place allow users to search for stocks of interest which reach beyond the popular companies section. Our news feed updates with the latest news published from multiple trusted news source. Our ambition is to continently enhance this page to be a reliable fiat on ramp to the stock market as well as crypto assets. - Technologies used; Visual Studio Code, Slack, Zoom, Finnhub API, News API and Api.Polygon.
